## Mesen

模拟器软件，需要下载，支持Linux。

Mesen is a multi-system emulator (NES, SNES, Game Boy, Game Boy Advance, PC Engine, SMS/Game Gear, WonderSwan) for Windows, Linux and macOS.

* https://www.mesen.ca/
* https://github.com/SourMesen/Mesen2/
